Our Story

How It All Began

We met on a rainy Tuesday at a coffee shop in San Francisco — both reaching for the last copy of the same novel. What started as a laugh over a shared literary taste turned into hours of conversation, and before we knew it, the coffee shop was closing around us.

Three years, two continents, and one very poorly timed knee injury later (Amir's proposal at the Eiffel Tower required a little improvisation), we're finally here — inviting the people we love most to witness us begin our greatest adventure together.

We can't wait to celebrate with you.
