Our Story

How It All Began

We met at a friend's party on a summer evening in Stockholm. We talked for hours, about our lives, our university years, our families and how we were raised, politics and more. Time flew fast.

More than two years later, two people from two different continents, and a proposal in the middle of Djurgården, we're finally here — inviting the people we love the most to witness us begin our life together.

We can't wait to celebrate with you!

Transportation (Church to Venue)

For guests coming from further away, we will arrange a bus from Christ the King's Church to Kockagården after the ceremony. The bus departs at approximately 14:25 — if you need a seat, please let us know when you fill in your RSVP form.

After the party, night buses run from Kungsbacka toward Gothenburg Central. We'd suggest finding a hotel close to a bus stop so you can get home as easily as possible after the celebrations. For up-to-date schedules, please use the Västtrafik app.
